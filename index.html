What is a Short Term Loan?

A short term loan lets you borrow a small amount of money to help you cover expenses until your next paycheque. These loans are designed to be short-term solutions for urgent financial needs. If you’re facing ongoing financial struggles, we strongly encourage speaking with a licensed credit counsellor to explore long-term options.

Is a Short Term Loan Right for Me?

When used responsibly, a short term loan can help in a financial pinch. However, it’s important to understand the risks. Short term loans are not meant for regular or long-term borrowing and can lead to more debt if misused.

Always consider your financial situation. If you find yourself borrowing again right after paying off a loan, a short term loan may not be the best option for you.

How Can I Use a Short Term Loan Responsibly?

  • ✅ Only borrow what you know you can repay on time.
  • ✅ Make sure the loan is a temporary solution, not a recurring one.
  • ✅ Choose lenders that are transparent and follow responsible lending practices.
  • ✅ Ask questions if you’re unsure about any fees or the repayment process.
  • ❌ Don’t borrow just to cover regular monthly expenses.
  • ❌ Don’t take out a new loan to pay off an old one.
  • ❌ Don’t borrow if you’re unsure you can repay it.

If you’re struggling with debt, consider speaking with a qualified debt advisor.
